resolution_scope: multiclass_item_equip_root_cause_and_fix

resolution_fact_5: The remaining blocker was the helper-layer class-bit translation, not the hook seam. resolution_fact_6: The fix was to align client item mask translation with the live ITEMINFO.Classes layout: playable class bits 1..16 map as 1 << (class_id - 1), with playable mask 0x0000ffff.
resolution_fact_7: After that translation fix, the late chest-slot gate override became a true authoritative overlap check, and the reproduce succeeded: Barbed Ringmail could be chest-equipped on Testmage through the live ROF2 client.
